One Wheel

About One Wheel

The One Wheel is an all terrain moterized skateboard that allows riders to move freely around their enviornment. With a standard heavy-duty go-cart wheel at the center, the One Wheel is able to take on any obsacle that might be in your way. Its tilt-to-go controls and it's ability to stop on the dime, the One Wheel is easy to pick up and very fun to ride.
